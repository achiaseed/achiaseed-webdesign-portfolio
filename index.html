Hi there, I'm Ashley!

I'm a UX/UI designer from in Brea, CA. In my heart lies a passion for designing, wireframing, prototyping, and serving others in any way I can. I love seeing the progression of a project from the very beginning and the changes it undergoes. It's a joy to see it morph and take shape. Like watching a child grow up, you grow fond and create a bond with those who helped to "nurture" it.

I have a black and white fluffy tuxedo cat named Shadow. He is the absolute fluff of my life! Animals have the uncanny ability to melt my already soft heart. Previously, I served on the cabinet board for Biola Hula Halau, which I absolutely love doing. I choreograph, lead, dance and teach whenever I can.

Whenever I can, I love going to Disneyland (pre-pandemic, of course!), discovering the latest foodie spots, and volunteering to help kitties at my local adoption center!
